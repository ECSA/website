What ECSA Is All About

We are an association dedicated to enriching the university experience of residents. Whether that be through community events, advocacy, or maintaining our community house, the East Campus Students' Association is striving to make your residence stay some of the best times of your university life. Please don't hesitate to contact us using our Contact Us page or Comment Box if you think we can improve in any way.
