About ECSA

 

This is the website for the East Campus Students' Association (ECSA). All residents of East Campus Village (ECV) are automatically a member of ECSA. Each member of ECSA pays a $41 annual association fee which is included with the first month of rent. $36 of this goes towards the ECSA budget, and the remaining $5 goes to the RHA.

 

The ECSA Executive consists of a President and three Vice Presidents (VP).

  • The President is responsible for coordinating with the other Executives to ensure ECSA functions smoothly.
  • The VP Finance is in charge of managing the ECSA budget throughout the year.
  • The VP Communication is in charge of communicating important information to ECV residents.
  • The VP House is responsible for maintaining ECSA House and coordinating the ECSA Volunteers.

 

We host various awesome events throughout the year, such as BBQs, brunches, games nights, movie nights, holiday events, etc. Join our Facebook Group or follow us on Twitter where we post information about upcoming ECSA events, and various other information about living in ECV.

 

We have a community centre, known as ECSA House, which is located at 11036-88 Ave. All ECV residents can have 24/7 access to this amazing house by filling out this Door Code Form. The ECSA House will be open at various times throughout each week for volunteer hours. During this time, feel free to drop by and use some of our services. Here's more information about Volunteering for ECSA. If you're interested in becoming a volunteer, you can apply here. All volunteers are greatly appreciated!
