ECSA House

East Campus Village has a community centre, known as ECSA House, which is located at 11036-88 Ave. This three storey house is an awesome place to hang out, and it's also where ECSA hosts most of its events and provides useful services.

 

ECSA members can get 24/7 access to ECSA House by filling out this Door Code Form. ECSA members can invite guests into ECSA House, but any non-ECSA members are not allowed to be in the house alone. Please always be respectful of the people and property in ECSA House.

 

If you would like to book ECSA House for your own private or public event, feel free to fill out this Book ECSA House Form. Keep in mind that alcohol is not allowed in ECSA House, and you must clean up any messes you make.

 

The main floor contains

  • An awesome 50 inch plasma TV with DVD player
  • Multiple comfy couches
  • A kitchen with fridge, oven, and microwave
  • The volunteer office
  • A router with free wifi

 

The upper floor contains

  • A small reading room
  • Study rooms

 

The basement contains

  • A pool table
  • Foosball table
  • A ping pong table
