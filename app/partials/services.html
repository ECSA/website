ECSA Services

There are various services that ECSA provides to ECV residents, other than hosting events. All our services are provided in ECSA House, and some services are only available during volunteer hours.

 

One service that is offered during volunteer hours is printing and scanning for only 5 cents per page. This is a much cheaper price than various places on campus!

 

The other main service offered during volunteer hours is access to various rental items. A deposit might be required, which will be returned when the item is returned

  • Vacuum cleaners (OneCard as deposit)
  • Air mattresses ($5 deposit)
  • Cleaning supplies
  • Board Games
  • DVDs
