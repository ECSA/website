Volunteer Schedule

This is the calendar for ECSA House volunteer office hours. During these times, the Open sign will be on, and there will be a volunteer in ECSA House. Feel free to drop by to use our services, or donate any bottle/can recycling during this time. If you find that the house is not open when it should be, please either email the VP House (ecsahouse@gmail.com), or let us know in our Comment Box.
